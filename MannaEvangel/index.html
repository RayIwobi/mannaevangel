Welcome to Our Church

At Manna Evangel Faith Assembly, we are a vibrant community dedicated to spreading the gospel, love, kindness, and faith. Here, you'll find a warm and welcoming atmosphere where everyone is valued and accepted just as they are. Whether you're exploring your spirituality, seeking fellowship, or simply curious about what we offer, you are always welcome!

BRAND NEW KIDNEYS

My case was terrible and looking really bad, a kidney transparent seems to be the way out, but the money to do that was scary! Then a healing service was announced in Evangel Bauchi Church. At the service the man of God prayed for me not knowing how severe my case was, but alas God turned my captivity around and gave me two brand new kidneys! The next time I went for check up even the consultants were shocked to see a miraculous reversal for Good. Today am OK, no symptoms, no pain, I am made whole completely.
Mummy J. Bauchi Branch

GOD OF MIRACLES

After my marriage my wife could not conceive, we waited and prayed, no way, until we attended a Special program organized for those trusting God for the fruit of the womb in Evangel Headquarters Church, Gombe. After the program my wife conceived and we had our first baby! God indeed answers prayers.
Bro. ZH Evangel School Staff

HEALED COMPLETELY

I am a product of divine intervention. I suffered from strange illness; invisible Lizard like creatures crawls all over my body for months, medically they could not discover anything and all I tried failed! At a healing service organized by Evangel Headquarters the man of God by prophetic utterance called out my case specifically and right at that moment all strange crawling ceased! That single experience has turned my life around, today I bear witness across Nations testifying to God's goodness.
Rev. George Ndukwe Eleke Ogele Church Member

Our Services

Church Services

Worship

In our worship services, we create an atmosphere where hearts are stirred, souls are refreshed, and spirits are renewed. Worship at Manna Evangel Faith Assembly is characterized by authenticity and sincerity.

Baptism

Baptism holds a central place in the spiritual journey of believers at Manna Evangel Faith Assembly. It symbolizes not only a public declaration of one's faith but also a profound spiritual transformation.

Exhortation

Exhortation plays a vital role in the life of the community at Manna Evangel Faith Assembly. It is the practice of encouraging, uplifting, and motivating one another to live out our faith boldly and faithfully.
