Ministeries under the Church

Ministries under the Church encompasses a diverse array of specialized functions and initiatives aimed at serving the congregation and broader community, each tailored to meet specific needs and promote spiritual growth. These ministries operate within the framework of the church, guided by its overarching mission and values. From outreach programs to conventional school, Apostolic Family Group, each ministry plays a vital role in fostering fellowship, compassion, and personal development among believers. Together, they form a tapestry of support and service, reflecting the diverse talents and passions of the church body while collectively advancing the shared vision of faith, love, and service.



Evangel Group of Schools

Established in the year 1996 by express divine direction received from the Lord by the Presiding Pastor to; "Give qualitative Christ centered education" to the children of this generation. With few students at inception the school now has two campuses each having the nursery and primary and secondary school. At the church campus additional programs: Accelerated Christian Education and "Switch on School" (SOS) are running concurrently at the rpimary and secondary schools respectively. Our children are trailblazers and pacesetters in the city of Gombe and North East in general; concurrently 3 of our students are on scholarchip exchange program in the United States of America.

Evangel free School

In a bid to give back to the society as part of our social responsibility and mission mandate, 5 Free Schools has been established at five centers cutting across four states (Gombe,Yobe, Bauchi and Plateau) to reach out to the less privileged so they can also partakes in the mandate of qualitative Christ centered Education.


Evangel Leadership Bible School

The Training arm of the Commission providing quality ministerial training to the body of Christ in Gombe at large in Basic Theological Training and School of Ministry exposure, to help equip the saints for the work of the Ministry. At the Last count close to 150 have graduated from the ELBS, Faculty cut’s across the major denominations in town. Admission is in January and graduation by December for certificate course. The interesting thing about the school is that no fees is charged at all.

Evangel Missions

Missions to the Nations is one the major trust of our Ministry. Every June of each year we focus on Missions and create awareness in cross-cultural Missions. Currently we support Missionaries across the Nation. A family is currently in foreign missions in Sierra Leon doing great exploit for the Lord..

Evangel Outreach and Church Planting Dept

Tract distribution and Street Evangelism, open air mini-crusades and subsequent discipleship of converts and Church planting are part of our flagship programs of our Ministry. The Outreach Department has great responsibility to deliver on this mandate annually in view of the determination of the Ministry to plant a total of 50 Churches by the end of the year 2025.


Apostolic Family Group (AFG)

In line with apostolic practice of the Early Church, breaking bread from house to house; the Ministry has adopted the model for effective mobilizing the laity for holistic ministry through close knitted fellowship.

Gombe December Prayer Camp

Annually, Strategic prayer and fasting program takes the center stage as we round up the year. The program has provided intercessors and prayer warriors, indeed the entire membersgip to do great exploits in prayer and gets miraculous answers to prayers.

Jesus Student Congress

Catching the younger Generations at their prime is a key mandate we make a reality by the gathering of Secondary school age students annually in a convention. The programs comes up August /September each year at both the Jos and Gombe branch. Great testimonies abounds each year from the outcomes of the programs
