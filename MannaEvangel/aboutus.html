About Us

At Manna Evangel Faith Assembly, we are a vibrant community dedicated to spreading love, kindness, and faith. Here, you'll find a warm and welcoming atmosphere where everyone is valued and accepted just as they are. Whether you're exploring your spirituality, seeking fellowship, or simply curious about what we offer, you are always welcome!



At Manna Evangel Faith Assembly, we are also passionate about serving others and making a difference in the world. Whether through outreach programs, community service initiatives, or missions work, we are dedicated to extending God's love and compassion to those in need. By actively engaging with the needs of our local and global communities, we aim to be agents of positive change and transformation.

Our Vision

Preach, teach, reach the whole world with the wholesome Word of eternal kingdom impact

Our Mission

To reach the nations through strategic evangelism, discipleship, leadership development, prayer for revivals, planting churches and establishing Christian educational institutions and cross-cultural missions


Our History

THE GENESIS:

In the year 1981 a handful of zealous Christian Youth Corpers serving the Nation in Gombe Township met regularly to have constant fellowship weekly at the residence of Brother Clement Anegbe (late) who was also on Youth Service with Nasara Motors then. On completion his compulsory one year youth service he took up an appointment with the Federal College of Education Technical Gombe as an instructor at the Technical Dept. of the college.

Our Services

Church Services

Worship

In our worship services, we create an atmosphere where hearts are stirred, souls are refreshed, and spirits are renewed. Worship at Manna Evangel Faith Assembly is characterized by authenticity and sincerity.

Baptism

Baptism holds a central place in the spiritual journey of believers at Manna Evangel Faith Assembly. It symbolizes not only a public declaration of one's faith but also a profound spiritual transformation

Exhortation

Exhortation plays a vital role in the life of the community at Manna Evangel Faith Assembly. It is the practice of encouraging, uplifting, and motivating one another to live out our faith boldly and faithfully.
